Community-Based Alternatives to Incarceration

Implementing community-based alternatives to incarceration, such as probation, parole, and diversion programs, can help low-risk offenders avoid the negative consequences of imprisonment while still holding them accountable for their actions. These programs can provide supervision, support, and interventions tailored to individual needs, reducing the likelihood of reoffending.
