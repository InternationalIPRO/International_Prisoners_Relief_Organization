Our Mission

IPRO is dedicated to providing comprehensive support to ex-offenders to help them reintegrate into society and reduce recidivism rates. Our mission is to offer rehabilitation, counseling, and practical assistance to those in need.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where former prisoners are successfully reintegrated into society, equipped with the skills and support needed to lead productive, fulfilling lives without returning to crime.

Our Values

We believe in compassion, justice, and the power of second chances. Our values guide our work and shape the way we engage with individuals, communities, and partners.

Our History

Founded in 2003, IPRO has been working tirelessly to support ex-offenders and promote effective rehabilitation and reintegration. Learn about our journey and the milestones we have achieved.
