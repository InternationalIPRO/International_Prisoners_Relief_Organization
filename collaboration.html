Collaboration and Partnerships

Effective reintegration of ex-offenders often requires collaboration with various stakeholders, including other organizations, governmental bodies, and community groups. IPRO actively engages in partnerships to enhance the support network for ex-offenders and to promote successful reintegration efforts.
